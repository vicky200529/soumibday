Happy Birthday, Soumili. 🤍💜🎂

Thank you.

Thank you har uss lamhe ke liye jab zindagi thodi mushkil thi, phir bhi tum meri life ka ek khoobsurat hissa bani rahi.

Aur thank you un saare lamhon ke liye jo shayad tumhare liye bas normal the, lekin meri yaadon ka hissa ban gaye.

Zindagi mein bahut log milte hain, bahut log chale bhi jaate hain.

Lekin kuch log roz baat na karke bhi kabhi paraye nahi hote. Tum unhi logon mein se ho.

Humari story perfect nahi thi... aur shayad isi liye itni sachchi thi.

Humne galtiyan bhi ki, hasi bhi baanti, kabhi ek dusre ko irritate bhi kiya.

Main tujhe gaali bhi deta tha... aur tu jaanti hai woh mera ajeeb sa love language tha.

Dil pe mat liya kar. 😂❤️

Aur haan...

"Oi age mat count kar moti... karon tumi cho peyecho!!!!" 😂

Pata nahi future mein hum kitna baat karenge. Shayad kam. Shayad bilkul nahi. Shayad zindagi humein alag raaston par le jaaye. Ya shayad yeh mera last message ho...

Aur sach kahun... Ye soch kar aaj bhi thoda ajeeb lagta hai.

Lekin ek baat maine samajh li hai... Kuch rishte saath rehkar nahi, yaadon mein rehkar bhi bahut khoobsurat hote hain.

Aur haan... Bichhadne ke toh hazaar bahane aur situations mil jaate hain. Lekin saath rehne ka sirf ek reason hota hai — Dil.

Use friendship bolo, love bolo, ya relationship... Naam alag ho sakte hain, lekin asli ehsaas hamesha ek hi hota hai.

Agar kabhi kisi din, kisi random gaane, kisi jagah, kisi baarish ki khushboo, ya kisi chhoti si baat par meri yaad aa jaaye...

Toh bas ek baar muskura dena. Mere liye itna hi kaafi hoga.

Main nahi jaanta meri yaad tumhari zindagi mein kitni der tak rahegi. Lekin itna zaroor jaanta hoon ki meri yaadon mein tum hamesha ek bahut hi khoobsurat jagah par rahogi.

Tumhari jagah meri life mein kabhi kisi aur ne li hi nahi... Aur shayad kabhi le bhi na paaye. Kuch log replace nahi hote, bas yaadon ka hissa ban jaate hain.

Bas ek dua hai... Tumhare saare sapne poore hon. Jo khushi tum dusron ko deti ho, woh tumhe usse bhi zyada mile.

Aur jab kabhi zindagi mushkil lage, toh kabhi ye mat sochna ki tumhari value kam hai. Tum jitni ho... utni hi kaafi ho.

Aur jis din tum khud ko us nazar se dekhna seekh jaogi, us din tumhe samajh aa jayega ki tum kitni special ho.

Happy Birthday, Meow. ❤️🌻

Khush rehna. Kyunki is duniya mein bahut log tumhe jaante honge... lekin bahut kam log honge jo dil se chahenge ki tum hamesha muskurati raho. Main unmein se ek hoon.

Aur agar kabhi zindagi tumhe ye sochne par majboor kare ki kaun tha jo bina kisi matlab ke tumhari khushi ke liye dua karta tha... toh bas ek baar meri taraf dekh lena.

Shayad tab samajh aa jaaye... Kuch log sirf zindagi ka hissa nahi hote, woh zindagi ki sabse khoobsurat yaadon mein se ek ban jaate hain.

Take care... Always. 🤍
